## How the Eight Sleep Pod Works: Technology Behind the Magic

The Eight Sleep Pod isn't a mattress itself but rather a high-tech cover that fits over your existing mattress. Here's how the technology works:

### Active Grid Layer

The core of the Eight Sleep system is the Active Grid—a matrix of water tubes and sensors integrated into the mattress cover. This grid:

- Actively cools or heats each side of the bed between 55°F and 110°F
- Collects biometric data while you sleep (heart rate, respiratory rate, sleep stages)
- Provides a thin comfort layer that minimally alters your mattress feel

### The Hub

The water-based cooling/heating system is powered by the Hub, a bedside unit that:

- Pumps water through the Active Grid to maintain your chosen temperature
- Operates quietly (though not completely silent) throughout the night
- Contains a water reservoir that needs occasional refilling (typically every 2-3 months)

### AI-Powered Temperature Control

What truly sets Eight Sleep apart is its Autopilot feature, which:

- Learns your sleep patterns and temperature preferences over time
- Automatically adjusts temperatures throughout the night to match your sleep stages
- Creates different temperature profiles for each side of the bed
- Offers thermal alarms that gradually warm the bed to wake you naturally

The technology has only improved in the 2025 models, with faster temperature changes and more responsive algorithms that better predict your needs based on room temperature, time of day, and historical sleep data.
