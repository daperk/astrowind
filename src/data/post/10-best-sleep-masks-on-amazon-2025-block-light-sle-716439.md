## What Makes a Great Sleep Mask?

Before diving into specific products, let's establish what separates an average mask from a sleep-transforming tool:

**Complete Blackout**: The primary purpose of any sleep mask is to block 100% of light. Even small light leaks can disrupt melatonin production and keep you awake.

**Comfort for Your Sleep Position**: Side sleepers need different features than back sleepers. Pressure points that are barely noticeable when falling asleep can become painful hours later.

**Breathable Materials**: Nobody wants to wake up with a sweaty face. Quality materials like silk provide cooling comfort throughout the night.

**Secure Yet Gentle Fit**: A mask that's constantly shifting defeats its purpose, but too-tight straps cause headaches and hair breakage.

**Durability**: A great sleep mask should withstand nightly use and regular cleaning without losing shape or function.
